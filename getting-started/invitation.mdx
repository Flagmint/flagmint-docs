# Inviting Team Members

Collaborate with your team by inviting members to your FlagMint organization. This guide shows you how to send invitations, manage roles, and help new members get started.

---

## Overview

Inviting team members to FlagMint is simple:

1. **Send an invitation** - Invite one person or multiple people at once
2. **They receive an email** - Your teammate gets an invitation link
3. **They join your organization** - They create their account and start collaborating

Invitations are valid for **3 days** and can be sent to anyone with an email address.

---

## Who Can Invite Team Members?

- **Admins** can invite anyone and send bulk invitations
- **Editors** can invite individual team members
- **Viewers** cannot send invitations

---

## Inviting a Single Team Member

### From the Team Settings Page

1. **Navigate to Settings** - Click the Settings icon in the sidebar, then select **Team**
2. **Click "Invite Member"** - Find the button in the page header
3. **Fill in the invitation form:**
   - **Name** - Your teammate's full name
   - **Email** - Their work email address
   - **Role** - Choose Editor or Viewer (see roles below)
4. **Click "Send Invite"** - The invitation email is sent immediately

### From the Dashboard

You can also invite team members directly from your dashboard:

1. Look for the **"Invite Team Member"** card on the right side
2. Click **"Invite Team Member"**
3. Fill in the same form as above
4. Click **"Send Invite"**

### What Happens Next?

- Your teammate receives an email with an invitation link
- The link is valid for 3 days
- They click the link to create their account
- Once they sign up, they'll appear in your team list

<Tip>
  **Need to invite multiple people?** At the bottom of the invitation form, click "Try bulk CSV import" to invite many team members at once.
</Tip>

## Inviting Multiple Team Members (Bulk Invite)

Save time by inviting multiple teammates in one go using a CSV file.

### Step 1: Prepare Your CSV File

**Option A: Download Our Template**

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click **"Bulk Invite (CSV)"** in the page header
3. Click **"Download CSV Template"** in the dialog
4. Open the file in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app

**Option B: Create Your Own**

Create a CSV file with these two columns:

```
name,email
John Doe,john@company.com
Jane Smith,jane@company.com
Mike Johnson,mike@company.com
```

**Important Notes:**
- First row must be the headers: `name,email`
- One person per row
- Maximum file size: 1 MB
- **Free plan:** 1 invitation at a time
- **Paid plans:** Up to 20 invitations at once

### Step 2: Upload Your CSV

1. Click **"Bulk Invite (CSV)"** from the Team Settings page
2. **Drag and drop** your CSV file onto the upload area, or click to browse
3. Wait a moment while your file is processed

### Step 3: Review and Assign Roles

You'll see a preview of everyone you're about to invite:

- **Green checkmark** ✅ - Ready to send
- **Red X** ❌ - There's an error (fix the email or name)
- **Yellow warning** ⚠️ - Duplicate email in your list

For each person, select their role:
- **Editor** - Can create and modify feature flags
- **Viewer** - Can only view flags

You can:
- Edit names or emails directly in the table
- Change roles using the dropdown
- Remove anyone by clicking the trash icon
- Click **"Back"** to upload a different file

When you're ready, click **"Continue"**.

### Step 4: View Results

After sending, you'll see a summary:

- **✅ Successful** - Invitation sent
- **🔵 Skipped** - Person is already a team member or was recently invited
- **❌ Failed** - Invalid email or other error

Click **"Done"** to close the dialog, or **"Send More"** to invite additional people.

<Note>
  Skipped invitations aren't errors - they just mean the person is already in your organization or already has a pending invitation.
</Note>

### Plan Limits

If you try to invite more people than your plan allows:

- **Free plan:** You can invite 1 person at a time
- **Paid plans:** You can invite up to 20 people at once

To invite more people, either:
- Send multiple batches
- Upgrade your plan

## Understanding Roles

When you invite someone, you assign them a role. Here's what each role can do:

### Editor

**Best for:** Developers, product managers, team leads

**Can do:**
- View all feature flags
- Create new feature flags
- Edit existing flags
- Toggle flags on/off
- Create and edit segments
- Manage environments
- View analytics

**Cannot do:**
- Manage billing
- Delete the organization
- Change other people's roles (only admins can)

### Viewer

**Best for:** Stakeholders, analysts, observers

**Can do:**
- View all feature flags and their status
- View segments
- View analytics and reports

**Cannot do:**
- Create or edit flags
- Toggle flags on/off
- Create or edit segments
- Invite team members

<Tip>
  You can always change someone's role later from the Team Settings page. Only admins can change roles.
</Tip>

## Accepting an Invitation

### If You've Been Invited

When someone invites you to their FlagMint organization, here's what to do:

1. **Check your email** - Look for an email from FlagMint with the subject "You've been invited to [Organization Name]"
2. **Click the invitation link** - This takes you to the sign-up page
3. **Review the invitation details:**
   - Organization you're joining
   - Your assigned role
   - Who invited you
4. **Choose how to sign up:**

### Option 1: Sign Up with Google

1. Click **"Continue with Google"**
2. Select your Google account
3. You're all set! You'll be redirected to the dashboard

### Option 2: Sign Up with Email and Password

1. Enter your **full name** (pre-filled from the invitation)
2. Create a **password**:
   - At least 8 characters
   - Include uppercase and lowercase letters
   - Include at least one number
3. **Confirm your password**
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email to verify your account

### After Signing Up

- You'll have access to your organization immediately
- Your role determines what you can do
- You can start collaborating on feature flags right away

<Warning>
  Invitation links expire after 3 days. If your link has expired, ask your teammate to send a new invitation.
</Warning>

## Managing Your Team

### Viewing Your Team Members

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. You'll see a list of all members with:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Role
   - When they joined

### Removing a Team Member

**Only admins can remove team members.**

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Find the team member in the list
3. Click the **trash icon** next to their name
4. Confirm the removal
5. They'll immediately lose access to your organization

## Common Questions

### How long are invitations valid?

Invitations expire after **3 days**. If someone doesn't accept in time, you'll need to send a new invitation.

### Can I resend an invitation?

Currently, you'll need to send a new invitation if the old one expires. We're working on adding a "resend" option.

### What if someone doesn't receive the invitation email?

Ask them to:
- Check their spam/junk folder
- Search their email for "flagmint"
- Check with their IT department about email filtering

If they still haven't received it after a few minutes, try sending a new invitation.

### Can I invite someone who already has a FlagMint account?

Yes! If they already have an account, they'll be added to your organization when they accept the invitation.

### Can the same person be in multiple organizations?

Yes, one person can be a member of multiple FlagMint organizations with different roles in each.

### What happens if I try to invite someone twice?

You'll see a "skipped" status if:
- They're already a member
- They have a pending invitation that hasn't expired
- You included their email twice in a bulk upload

This prevents duplicate invitations and doesn't count as an error.

### Can I change someone's role after inviting them?

Yes! Admins can change any team member's role from the Team Settings page. The change takes effect immediately.

### Do invitations cost anything?

No, sending invitations is free. You only pay for your subscription based on your plan.

### Can I cancel an invitation?

Currently, invitations expire automatically after 3 days. We're working on adding a manual cancellation option.

## Tips for Success

### For Sending Invitations

1. **Use clear names** - Use full names so team members can recognize each other
2. **Double-check emails** - Typos in email addresses will fail
3. **Assign appropriate roles** - Give people the access they need (but not more)
4. **Plan for growth** - Consider your plan limits before bulk inviting

### For Accepting Invitations

1. **Act quickly** - Don't wait until the last day; links expire in 3 days
2. **Use a strong password** - If not using Google sign-in
3. **Check the organization name** - Make sure it's the right team
4. **Save your login info** - You'll need it to access FlagMint later

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid or expired token" Error

**Why this happens:**
- The invitation link is older than 3 days
- The link was already used
- The link was copied incorrectly

**Solution:** Ask your teammate to send a new invitation.

### Can't Upload CSV File

**Why this happens:**
- File is larger than 1 MB
- File isn't a CSV format
- File doesn't have the correct headers

**Solution:**
- Make sure your file is saved as `.csv`
- Use the template we provide
- Reduce the number of rows if the file is too large

### "Plan Limit Exceeded" Error

**Why this happens:**
- Free plans can only invite 1 person at a time
- Paid plans max out at 20 invitations per batch

**Solution:**
- Send invitations in smaller batches
- Upgrade your plan for higher limits

### Invitation Email Not Received

**Try these steps:**
1. Wait 5-10 minutes (emails can be delayed)
2. Check spam/junk folder
3. Search email for "flagmint" or the inviter's name
4. Ask your IT team if emails from flagmint.com are blocked
5. Request a new invitation to be sent
